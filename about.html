Cape Tables was started in 2018 by Andy, Matt, and Dewan in the Southern Suburbs of Cape Town.

Matt is originally from Los Angeles and is studying Mechatronics Engineering at UCT with Dewan. He has a background in carpentry and used to work in the film industry back home.

Dewan is in his final year at UCT and is also studying Mechatronics. He is from Bloemfontein, is an avid runner, and a pilot. I have heard his pilot’s voice before, it's good!
